In 2020 and 2021, there has been a sudden increase of recognition of the problems Black Americans face and have fought. Black Americans are subject to discrimination in the legal, police, education and other systems. In the past few years, there has been a sudden increase in hate crimes, discrimniation and inequalities towards Black Americans. Black Americans of all ages fear police harassment and discrimination solely because of their skin color.

TAKEAWAYS FROM THE MOVEMENT, PROTESTS, ETC

There are some key takeaways for people of all ages from this rise in activism for black lives and standing for equality.

  • Self-reflection and education is always encouraged.

  • The public has power to some extent but when we protest for a cause, things will happen and we will achieve them.

  • No one is ever alone; there will always be people willing to support you.

  • Racism is never okay and your experiences matter; your role is to report such events.

  • Recognize your privilege and aim to seek the same for those around you who do not have such privilege.
