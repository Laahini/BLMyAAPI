WHAT IS HAPPENING?

The COVID-19 Pandemic has hit the world hard in the past 2 years and many govenrment have struggled to find effective solutions to handle the crisis. The Coronavirus that caused this pandemic originate from China. The pandemic has affected several populations and families such as causing a loss of jobs, loss of loved ones, stress and impacts on daily life along with what is known as "normal". This in turn has created some to retaliate and resort to violence and hatred to Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders. These races have already faced discrimnation and prejudices like being labeled the "model minority" and other racist, derogatory terms that are stereotypical. This violence has occured in different forms but include shootings of Asian Americans and other cruel treatment. IN SIMPLER TERMS, the root cause is hatred to Asian Americans exemplified by the COVID-19 pandemic and its origins.
